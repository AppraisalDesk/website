The Real Cost of Appraisal Management

After 16 years of building and operating one of the nation's leading AMCs, we've seen firsthand what appraisal management really costs organizations:

Multiple Systems: Juggling separate platforms for ordering, payment processing, reporting, and compliance creates inefficiency and errors.

Manual Billing Processes: Spreadsheet invoices, check reconciliation, and payment tracking consume significant time and resources - often costing organizations over $70,000 annually in operational overhead.

Complex Appraiser Management: Vetting credentials, managing compliance, and matching orders to qualified appraisers takes expertise and constant oversight.
