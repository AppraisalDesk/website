LENDER SERVICE AGREEMENT

Appraisaldesk.com LLC d/b/a Appraisal Desk

Effective Date: Upon Electronic Acceptance

This Lender Service Agreement ("Agreement") supplements the Platform Terms of Service and establishes specific terms for lenders using the Appraisal Desk platform.

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

1.1 Platform Access

We provide you access to the Platform for submitting, managing, and receiving appraisal orders in connection with your lending operations.

1.2 Order Submission Methods

You may submit orders through:

  • Direct submission via web interface
  • API integration with your Loan Origination Software (LOS)

Orders cannot be submitted via email or other methods not directly integrated with the Platform.

2. PAYMENT OPTIONS AND BILLING

2.1 Payment Model Configuration

Your payment model is configured per AMC partnership in the Platform interface. You may use different payment models for different AMC relationships:

Option 1: Inclusive Platform Fee

  • Platform fee included in total order amount
  • We collect full amount and distribute to service providers
  • Single payment for complete service

Option 2: Separate Platform Billing

  • Platform fee billed separately
  • Requires ACH authorization on file
  • Service provider payments handled by Platform or AMC partner

Option 3: AMC Pays Platform Fee

  • Your designated AMC pays our platform fees
  • You pay only appraisal service fees
  • No platform billing to you

2.2 Order Pricing

Each order will specify:

  • Total order amount
  • Platform fee (if applicable)
  • Selected payment option
  • Payment responsibility

2.3 Auto-Pay Requirements

For Option 2, you must maintain:

  • Valid ACH-enabled bank account
  • Current authorization for automatic debits
  • Sufficient funds for scheduled payments

3. AMC NETWORK AND ROUTING

3.1 Network Access

You gain access to our nationwide network of qualified AMCs and appraisers, all of whom maintain:

  • Required state licensing/registration
  • Errors and omissions insurance
  • Demonstrated competency
  • USPAP compliance

3.2 Preferred AMC Partners

You may designate preferred AMCs within our network.

3.3 Alternative Routing Rights

We reserve the right to route orders to alternative qualified AMCs when:

  • Preferred AMCs lack capacity
  • Geographic coverage is unavailable
  • Turnaround requirements cannot be met
  • Business needs require alternative routing

This ensures order completion and prevents delays.

3.4 No Direct Appraiser Contact

Per federal regulations, all appraiser communication must go through the designated AMC. Direct lender-to-appraiser communication regarding valuation is prohibited.

4. APPRAISAL INDEPENDENCE COMPLIANCE

4.1 Federal Requirements

You acknowledge and agree to comply with:

  • Dodd-Frank Act provisions
  • Interagency Appraisal and Evaluation Guidelines
  • Truth in Lending Act (TILA) requirements
  • All state appraisal independence requirements

4.2 Platform Safeguards

The Platform enforces independence through:

  • Restricted communication channels
  • AMC firewall between you and appraisers
  • Audit trails for all communications
  • Prohibition on value influence

4.3 Your Responsibilities

You remain responsible for:

  • Overall regulatory compliance
  • Proper use of appraisal reports
  • Loan underwriting decisions
  • Consumer protection compliance

5. REFUNDS AND CANCELLATIONS

5.1 Full Refunds Available When:

  • Order cancelled before work begins
  • AMC cannot complete through no fault of yours
  • Processing fees may be deducted

5.2 Partial Refunds Available When:

  • Order cancelled after work begins but before completion
  • Amount based on work completed
  • Processing fees non-refundable

5.3 No Refunds When:

  • Order completed according to specifications
  • Cancellation due to your changed requirements
  • Borrower fails to provide access or cooperation
  • You reject completed appraisal for business reasons

6. LOS INTEGRATION (Optional)

6.1 API Integration

We may provide API integration with your LOS system, subject to:

  • Technical compatibility assessment
  • Additional integration fees
  • Separate API license agreement
  • Compliance with our technical specifications

6.2 Technical Requirements

You must maintain:

  • Compatible LOS system
  • Reliable API connectivity
  • Adequate security measures
  • Designated technical contact

6.3 Integration Support

We provide:

  • Initial setup assistance
  • Technical documentation
  • Reasonable troubleshooting support
  • Update notifications

7. LENDER-SPECIFIC OBLIGATIONS

7.1 You Must:

  • Maintain all required lending licenses
  • Provide accurate property and borrower information
  • Respond timely to clarification requests
  • Review completed appraisals promptly
  • Make lending decisions independently

7.2 You Must Not:

  • Attempt to influence appraisal values
  • Communicate directly with appraisers about valuation
  • Share platform access credentials
  • Use appraisals for unauthorized purposes
  • Misrepresent order urgency or type

8. SERVICE LEVELS

8.1 Platform Availability

We target 99.9% platform uptime during business hours, excluding scheduled maintenance.

8.2 Order Processing

Order assignment timing depends on AMCs or Appraisal Desk Departments inviting appraisers in a timely manner. The Platform encourages appraisers to review and accept invitations within 2 hours of receipt. Actual assignment timing varies based on AMC response times and appraiser availability.

8.3 Support Response

  • Critical issues: 4 hour response target
  • Standard issues: 1 business day response target
  • General inquiries: 2 business day response target
