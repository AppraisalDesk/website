APPRAISER SERVICE AGREEMENT

Appraisaldesk.com LLC d/b/a Appraisal Desk

Effective Date: Upon Electronic Acceptance

This Appraiser Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Appraisaldesk.com LLC d/b/a Appraisal Desk ("Company," "Platform", "we," "us," or "our") and the appraiser accepting this Agreement ("Appraiser," "you," or "your").

By accepting this Agreement, you acknowledge that you have previously accepted the Appraisal Desk Terms of Service, which governs your use of the platform. This Agreement establishes additional professional obligations specific to your role as an appraiser in our network.

1. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR RELATIONSHIP

1.1 Contractor Status

You agree to work as an independent subcontractor to the Company for the completion of appraisal orders received through the Platform. This is not an employment relationship, partnership, or joint venture.

1.2 Business Independence

  • The arrangement between Company and Appraiser is nonexclusive
  • There is no guarantee of any volume of business
  • You may accept orders from other sources and platforms
  • You maintain your own business, licenses, equipment, and methods
  • You control your own schedule and work methods, subject to order requirements

1.3 Tax Obligations

Company will issue Form 1099-NEC for all payments made during the calendar year. You are solely responsible for all applicable taxes on payments received.

2. CRITICAL PROFESSIONAL REQUIREMENTS

2.1 USPAP Compliance

All appraisals must comply with current Uniform Standards of Professional Appraisal Practice (USPAP) standards and requirements.

2.2 Appraiser Independence

You are prohibited from communicating directly with lenders or clients regarding any order initiated through the Platform. All communications must flow through the designated AMC or Company.

2.3 Objective Valuation Standards

You agree to provide your professional and objective opinion of value without influence from:

  • Homeowner or real estate agent estimates
  • Purchase prices in purchase agreements
  • Unverified information or hearsay
  • Any party with an interest in the transaction

2.4 Order Response Requirements

  • You must respond to order invitations within the time specified in the order, with a maximum of 24 hours
  • You must respond to revision requests within 48 hours
  • You must provide realistic completion timeframes upon order acceptance

2.5 Property Inspection

You must personally inspect the subject property for every order you accept. If supervising a trainee, you must inspect the property and may sign as Supervisory Appraiser only if you performed the inspection.

3. INSURANCE REQUIREMENT

3.1 Mandatory Coverage

CRITICAL REQUIREMENT: You must maintain errors and omissions (E&O) insurance with coverage limits equal to or greater than the value of properties being appraised.

3.2 Coverage Verification

  • You must provide current certificates of insurance before receiving orders
  • You must immediately notify Company of any coverage changes
  • Company reserves the right to verify coverage and suspend order assignments for inadequate coverage

4. AMC NETWORK PARTICIPATION

4.1 Network Orders

This Agreement serves as an umbrella agreement covering orders from all AMCs participating in the Appraisal Desk network.

4.2 AMC-Specific Requirements

  • You will be notified through the Platform when new AMCs join with additional terms
  • Additional AMC-specific requirements are automatically incorporated into this Agreement
  • When conflicts arise, Appraisal Desk terms take precedence unless specifically agreed otherwise

4.3 Opt-Out Rights

You may opt out of receiving orders from specific AMCs by contacting Company through the Platform contact form. You understand that opting out may reduce available order volume.

5. GEOGRAPHIC COVERAGE AND COMPETENCY

5.1 Coverage Areas

You must specify and maintain current information regarding:

  • Geographic coverage areas
  • Property types and specializations
  • Current licensing jurisdictions

5.2 Competency Standards

You agree to:

  • Only accept orders within your areas of competency
  • Update your coverage areas and specializations as they change
  • Decline orders outside your expertise or availability
  • Maintain all required licenses in good standing

6. PROFESSIONAL STANDARDS AND ACKNOWLEDGMENTS

By accepting this Agreement, you acknowledge and agree that:

  • You understand and will comply with all Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform Act requirements
  • You are prohibited from speaking to lenders/clients regarding orders from the Platform
  • If contacted by a lender/client, you will notify both the AMC handling the order and Appraisal Desk immediately
  • You will provide your professional and objective opinion of value
  • You will not allow purchase prices to influence your opinion of value
  • You will not allow estimated values or target values to influence your opinion
  • You will not allow hearsay or unverified information to influence valuations
  • You will maintain confidentiality of all customer and consumer information
  • You will protect confidential information in accordance with applicable federal and state laws
  • Your work may be subject to quality review and performance monitoring
  • You may be removed from the approved appraiser list at any time without prior notification
  • You will comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations
  • There is no guarantee of any volume of business from the Platform

7. APPRAISER INDEPENDENCE CERTIFICATION

7.1 Certification Attachment

Company and its AMC partners attach an "Appraiser Independence Certificate" to every appraisal report submitted through the Platform, certifying compliance with all appraiser independence regulations including TILA, Dodd-Frank, FHA, FNMA, FHLMC, and applicable state regulations.

7.2 Breach of Independence

You acknowledge that any breach of appraiser independence requirements or the professional commitments in this Agreement may cause significant damages to Company, AMCs, and lenders, for which you may be held liable.

8.1 Work Product

Company owns all appraisal reports and related data created through the Platform, including all intellectual property rights therein.

8.2 Your Information

You retain ownership of your business information and personal data, subject to the license rights granted in the Terms of Service.

8.3 Data Usage

You grant Company a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license to use work product and data as necessary for business operations, regulatory compliance, and service provision.

9. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

9.1 Service Liability

Company assumes zero liability for your professional performance, quality of services, or disputes with AMCs or lenders. You are solely responsible for your work product.

9.2 Indemnification

You agree to indemnify and hold harmless Company from any claims arising from your performance, violation of this Agreement, or violation of applicable laws or regulations.

10. TERM AND TERMINATION

10.1 Term

This Agreement remains in effect until terminated by either party or until your account is deactivated.

10.2 Termination Effects

Upon termination:

  • Your access to new orders ceases immediately
  • You must complete pending orders or arrange reassignment
  • All data ownership and confidentiality provisions survive
  • Payment obligations for completed work survive
