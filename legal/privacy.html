What we do: We operate a software platform that helps manage appraisal orders and processes payments between lenders, AMCs, and appraisers.

Information we collect: Your business details, payment info, property data, and platform usage. We also receive borrower information from lenders for order processing.

How we use it: To process orders, handle payments, provide customer support, and meet legal requirements.

Who we share with: Other parties involved in your orders (lenders, AMCs, appraisers), service providers who help run our platform, and government agencies when required by law.

Your rights: You can access your information, opt out of certain sharing, and control marketing communications.

Important: Deletion of user account data does not include removal from historical order records, which must be maintained for regulatory compliance.

Questions? Contact privacy@appraisaldesk.com

1. WHO THIS APPLIES TO

This policy covers everyone who uses our platform or receives communications about orders:

Business Users: Lenders, AMCs, appraisal departments, and appraisers

Consumers: Borrowers, property contacts, and real estate agents receiving order updates

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

From You Directly:

  • Business information (name, company, licenses, contact details)
  • Payment information (bank accounts, credit cards)
  • Tax ID numbers (for 1099 reporting)

From Your Orders:

  • Property addresses and details
  • Loan information and borrower data
  • Appraisal reports and communications

Automatically:

  • How you use our platform (pages viewed, features used)
  • Device information (IP address, browser type)
  • Cookies and similar technologies

From Others:

  • Professional license verification
  • Information from loan origination systems
  • Public property records

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • Process and manage appraisal orders
  • Collect and distribute payments
  • Issue tax forms (1099s) to contractors
  • Provide customer support and platform improvements
  • Meet legal and regulatory requirements
  • Prevent fraud and maintain security

4. WHO WE SHARE INFORMATION WITH

Order Participants: We share relevant information with lenders, AMCs, appraisers, and others involved in specific orders.

Service Providers: Companies that help us operate, including payment processors, technology providers, and security services.

Government Agencies: Regulators, law enforcement, and tax authorities when required by law.

Note: We don't sell your personal information to other companies for marketing purposes.

5. HOW LONG WE KEEP INFORMATION

  • Appraisal reports and order data: 7 years minimum, or until it is deemed irrelevant to hold a workfile record any longer.
  • Payment records: 7 years
  • Your info on a specific order: 7 years minimum, or until it is deemed irrelevant to hold a workfile record any longer.
  • Your account information: 3 years after you stop using our services
  • Communications: 7 years

6. HOW WE PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION

  • Encrypt data during transmission and storage
  • Multi-factor authentication for platform access
  • Regular security testing and monitoring
  • Secure data centers with restricted access
  • Background checks for employees handling sensitive data

If there's a data breach: We'll notify you within 60 days and notify regulators within 72 hours.

9. LEGAL COMPLIANCE

We follow these laws and regulations:

  • Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act: Federal financial privacy law
  • Dodd-Frank Act: Appraisal industry regulations
  • State appraisal board rules: In all states where we operate
  • State privacy laws: California, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, and others

Our Regulators: State appraisal boards, Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB), Federal Trade Commission (FTC), and state financial regulators.
